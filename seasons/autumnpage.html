Autumn is the time of the month where most trees start to lose their leaves after they have changed from an array of beautiful colors. The temperature slowly drops into the colder tempertures but for the most part it is still acceptable to wear a long sleave or a sweatshirt. In the Northern Hemisphere, the first day of Autumn begins on September 22. It is also arguablly the best time to take amazing photos of the vibrant nature.
